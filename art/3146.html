In March 2016, he released his sixth solo album, called 'The Winding Man', under his own Kajama label. Recorded in India and Nashville, it received acclaim for being a mix of rock, folk, country, and Americana. The BBC described it as "A confident, assured record, it shows a man who's decided to follow his muse in all its forms."
