Kindergarten Camp Kindergarten Camp is one of the very first children's music and learning programs started by the Frog, Ltd. company. The program has been in development since 1997. It is currently performed at The University of Missouri's Children's Creative Center, in Columbia, Missouri. The songs performed in kindergarten camp are primarily instrumental, using a variety of instruments, including xylophones, vibraphones, glockenspiels and mallet instruments. In 2006, Frog, Ltd. began production on a companion songbook to Kindergarten Camp, titled Kindergarten Classics. It features all the music currently used in Kindergarten Camp, plus a selection of standards like "Twinkle Twinkle Little Star" and "Seventy-Six Trombones". Kindergarten Classics has been published by Hal Leonard Corporation.
