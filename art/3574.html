Once under the Arch-Vile’s control, the victim can then raise and command a legion of Zombies as powerful as the King of Terror himself. Will you be able to defeat the Zombie Horde, or will they doom the Earth and enslave the Human race? Your mission is to defeat them, and you will find them in countless numbers in the abandoned village, from far off woods, and from the depths of Hell itself!
