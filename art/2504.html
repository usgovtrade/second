This season, on Alone With Ghosts, our cast featured people with disabilities in ways that were unprecedented for the show. The premiere episode featured Tae-Hwa, a deaf young woman who was kidnapped by a stranger. She was later killed when the kidnapper lost control and strangled her to death with a cable. To date, three of the episodes have been made available on Netflix in eight territories: Australia, New Zealand, the US, the UK, Sweden, Norway, Finland, and Denmark.
