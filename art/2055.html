The Sea Slug Slugger) is an album by trumpeter Art Farmer recorded in 1968 and released on the Blue Note label. Reception The Allmusic review by Scott Yanow stated "Trumpeter Art Farmer returns to his roots (tenor and alto saxophones) for a set of hard bop".
