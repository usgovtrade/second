The third single, "One Less Problem" was released on 3 March 2019. The band's second album, 'Stitches' was released on 13 June 2019. In December 2019, the band appeared as part of BBC Scotland's annual Hogmanay Live in Edinburgh with their single "Gin & Juice", a take on the Fats Domino song. They also released a video for the song "One Less Problem", released from their second album Stitches.
