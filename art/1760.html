I'd Never Do It To You Again (Wendy Matthews song) "I'd Never Do It to You Again" is a song co-written and recorded by American country music artist Wendy Matthews. It was released in September 1999 as the first single from the album The Great Escape. The song reached number 15 on the Billboard Hot Country Singles & Tracks chart. The song was written by Matthews and Mark D. Sanders. Content The narrator recalls that a man who cheated on his wife (and has since died) is being re-evaluated after "heaven". He comments on how surprised and hurt he would be, since, though he knew about the infidelity, he never "did it to you again." Music video The music video is directed by Steven Goldmann and premiered in late 1999. Chart performance "I'd Never Do It To You Again" debuted at number 62 on the U.S. Billboard Hot Country Singles & Tracks for the week of September 12, 1999.
