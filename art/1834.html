Wedding Jewelry Collection: There are two main types of wedding jewelry: the bridal set and the bridal party jewelry. Wedding bridal sets are jewelry designed specifically for your wedding, in some cases, for a particular event such as your engagement party or your bridal shower. Bridal sets are not necessarily the same jewelry, although they are designed to match your wedding dress, they can be worn together or separately. They are not a pair of accessories and therefore, they are not “wedding accessories”. Wedding bridal sets are very popular because they allow you to select a piece that best suits you and your style.
