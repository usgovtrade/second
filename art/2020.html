We're a Hot Mess with a mission to create a platform for writers to read great, interesting, and entertaining short fiction. We want to help writers of short stories get their short fiction out into the world. So, if you're looking for some interesting reading matter with a new short story every two weeks, please consider joining our community. Go to hotmessfiction.com and tell us what you think of the new site! “A Forkful of Bad Dudes” will be released by our first sponsor, Snarled Sip.
