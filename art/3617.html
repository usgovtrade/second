The Ultimate Shockwave: Winter Blast is a free iOS game in the Ultimate series. The game starts off as a remake of Winter Blast from Donkey Kong Country Returns and Donkey Kong Land. The final product has been upgraded to the Wii U version of the game, with upgraded visuals and HD audio, but lost the mini games and a few other elements. Reception It was downloaded over 2 million times in January 2016.
