Here is how it works: The Gas Daily and Barron's Composite reports provide an historical snapshot of prices and volumetric information. This is accomplished each day by asking gas traders to provide quotes for prices and volumes for the previous day. As a result of these phone calls, we receive an electronic version of the CA for the previous day. Because our goal is to achieve maximum accuracy, our methodology involves verifying with traders the information contained in the report.
