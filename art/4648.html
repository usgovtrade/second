Rugby sevens at the 2015 Pan American Games Rugby sevens at the 2015 Pan American Games in Toronto, Canada was held from July 11 to 12, 2015. In this sport the teams consists of four players instead of seven and play in two seven minute halves instead of fifteen. At the inaugural Pan American Games women's rugby was first introduced. The venue for the rugby tournament was University of Toronto Scarborough. Medal summary Medal table Medal events Qualification A total of 16 men's teams and 16 women's team will qualify to compete at the games. Each nation may enter a maximum of 6 teams per gender (a total of 20 teams). Men Women Participating nations A total of 5 countries have qualified rugby sevens squads.
