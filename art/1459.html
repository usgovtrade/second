It's Survivor Warfare week on the CFI podcast, and Rob and John are joined by Dr. Chris Mosby. Chris has been a paramedic for more than 10 years, and is a graduate of the Canadian Forces Paramedic Training School. He's been with the Paramedic Service of Waterloo Region since 2012, and he is now a critical care paramedic. In this episode, Rob, John, and Chris discuss: The basics of survival and evacuation The importance of CPR and C-spine immobilization Defensive medicine What the paramedic uses in an urban scenario Tactical operations and how paramedics can fit into them Chris' training experience with firearms What Chris has done since he joined the CFS Links: Chris' book on survival medicine: http://www.amazon.com/Do-Not-Try-Survival-Paramedic-ebook/dp/B00I6M6KMA/ Rob's book on survival medicine: http://amzn.to/2BcDlOo A good blog on "No Hero" type training: http://www.notharingourpatients.com/ The book on Chris' website: http://www.tacticalresuscitations.ca/ Follow Rob @rob_fox on Instagram, Twitter, and Facebook.
