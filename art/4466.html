Bhale Dongalu Bhale Dongalu is a 1987 Telugu action drama film, produced by Geetha Madhuri and V. Geetha Devi and directed by B. Gopal under the Sri Rajeev Productions banner. It stars Akkineni Nagarjuna, Radha in the lead roles and music composed by Sivaji Raja. Plot The film is about two brothers Baba Ram Reddy (Akkineni Nagarjuna) & Bhale Dongalu (Akkineni Nagarjuna), who are good friends. One day Baba Ram's sister Jaya (Srividya) elopes with Baba Ram's friend Siva Rao (Kota Srinivasa Rao) when Siva Rao is abducted by some smugglers. In that scuffle, they see Dongalu and take him too. However, Dongalu proves himself innocent and unites the brothers who are separated in childhood, and they start fighting against crime in future. But the bad guys use the help of Dongalu to kill Baba Ram, thus they capture Jaya. Baba Ram succeeds to escape and joins the force to nab Dongalu. What transpires next forms the crux.
