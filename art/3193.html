ainrun.com About the Author David M. Walker has been a member of the National Security Space community for more than 30 years. He has operated on manned spaceflight spacecraft for the Naval Research Laboratory, the University of Houston Space Center, and NASA Headquarters. He currently serves as the Chief of Mission Integration in the U.S. Navy Space and Electronic Systems Center, and has developed a number of space safety measures and flight training programs for space-flown weapons systems. He also helped to develop NASA's flight safety program in the late 1970s and served as the Flight Safety Officer for the Space Shuttle flight STS-51D. Mr. Walker served as an assistant professor at the University of Houston and taught astronomy and space physics courses while working for NASA.
