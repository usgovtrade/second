Two Brains Are Better Than One At one point during this process, I found myself sitting at a small table in a library. While looking through a stack of papers for something completely unrelated, my eye fell upon some of my initial notes from the beginning of this project. I was surprised to find the information I had been working with since the early days of the project still applicable. The information was the results from an analysis done on the filmography of Stanley Kubrick. As much as I found his work brilliant, I found myself a bit underwhelmed with the list of people he has worked with. Then, this morning, I sat down and analyzed my own work and realized the same feeling. I did not have a large list of credits. However, when I thought about it a bit more, I found myself with quite a list. I may not be an elite director, but I am an elite filmmaker.
