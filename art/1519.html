I Will Destroy You”, a song that I wrote with my friend and vocalist Tia Ray which was featured on the video game “NHL 09.” I had a blast performing. It's always so nice to hear that an artist appreciates your work. Thank you Jody and the fans at the club for making my night! You can check out the performance video on the next page. Enjoy! We did do one thing that I haven't done before: A special acoustic performance with just the song "I Will Destroy You." This song was used as the entrance theme to the video game NHL 09 which features the New York Islanders. (Yes, you read that correctly. A hockey game was part of my entrance song.) It was a different experience being an audience and listening rather than performing. Thanks to Jody and her team for letting us do this and for helping me promote my music. My buddy Tia Ray did a guest vocal performance on a special acoustic version of my song "Falling Leaves" with David Brooke of The Shins. Tia gave me my first introduction to the "Boombox," a device that is used as an amp. This was my very first experience with a Boombox. I did take a pic of the gear, but it's hard to see my friend with the giant headphones on. I actually purchased a new "turntable" after this gig. It's a great addition for any budding DJs, I recommend it. You can have fun with your home entertainment set-up like Jody did, I think it's really cool. A couple of months later I got to take the stage to perform for another group of people. I was honored to be invited backstage to take a photo with Chris Brokaw of the band "The Shins." It was a fun experience having a few minutes to chill with the guys from the band and talk music and life. I was excited to have met them because Chris has the number 1 song in the country "The Greatest." I'm so thrilled for Chris because he is very talented. I'll be checking out The Shins "Campfire Songs" when it comes out. They are just plain awesome. I want to thank you for coming to my shows! This was my third time performing for the Long Island City crew, and it was just as awesome as the previous two times. We had some great people at the gig including the super talented Tia Ray and her friend Dave. This was my first time performing at "The S.O.B.'s." I had no idea that there were so many people here tonight for me, a small group of family and a few friends. I was nervous about performing in front of such a large group of people. I'll have to work on that because it makes for a better show when you can really connect with the audience. Thanks again for coming out to the show and supporting my music.
