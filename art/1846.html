Wildlife Conservancy Council (WCC) - Wildlife Refuges of the United States One of the most important and interesting wildlife projects in the US is the WCC. WCC helps to establish wildlife conservation programs and maintain a network of over 3,000 public wildlife refuges across the country. They help maintain habitat, which is essential for survival for so many native and migratory animals, including deer, elk, and moose. The WCC also publishes information for the public on wildlife conservation efforts. If you're looking for information about wildlife refuges in your area, or if you want to support the WCC, this is a good site to visit.
