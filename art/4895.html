F.C. Bravo FC Bravo is a sports club from San Marino, best known for its futsal team. It is one of the most successful teams in European futsal. Bravo has won the UEFA Futsal Cup four times and five Lega Nazionale Professionisti Futsal championships, as well as finishing runner-up a further two times. The team plays its home matches at Stadio Comunale, a multi-use stadium in the town of San Marino. The team's colors are red and black. Futsal history The club was founded in 2012 and was promoted to the first division after only one season, where it finished a league best sixth. In 2012–13, the club reached the quarter-final stage of the UEFA Futsal Cup after eliminating the favourites for the title SL Benfica in a two-legged match. In the following years, the team maintained a solid position in the first league, winning the league in 2013–14 and 2014–15 and reaching the final of the UEFA Futsal Cup in 2015–16. The club also won two championships and the domestic cup in 2016–17. The club was defeated by BGS Juventus in the final of the UEFA Futsal Cup. In 2017, the club won the domestic cup, also known as the Coca Cola Cup, with an 18–0 win over A.S.D. Città di Palermo, becoming the first club to accomplish this feat. The following year, the team became champion again, as well as achieving the fifth title in its history. The club defeated Sisport Višegrad in the final of the UEFA Futsal Cup, earning its fourth title. The club has since become known for its success in European competitions, defeating clubs such as Juventus, SpVgg Unterhaching, A.S. Montebelluna and F.C. Bidasoa before winning the UEFA Futsal Cup for the first time.
