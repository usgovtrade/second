7) Internationalisation


In a few years, we have become a significant player on the Spanish market, with our own offices in four out of the major cities, and branches in other regional capitals. We have also been active on a global level since 1994, when we launched our operations in the United States.

This has allowed us to acquire a solid reputation as an international company, with the result that we are one of the most awarded companies in our industry.


All our projects focus on new technologies, be it through the application of the latest methods in software design, or the implementation of new communication systems. In short, we always push the boundaries of technology and know-how, and this is our main competitive advantage. Our team of engineers, after all, is unrivalled in the world of information systems.

Company history


TECNOSSIS began in 1992, with the founding of the company by two partners, Carlos Carcas and Ricardo Cernadas. Carlos and Ricardo are well-known personalities in the information technology sector, and they have created a competitive company which represents the technological cutting edge, based on innovation and knowledge.


TECNOSSIS initially specialised in hardware and communications technology, and was soon expanding its range of activities, by supplying software. In 1993 it won the first award ever presented to a software developer, and since then our reputation as the leading IT firm has grown considerably.


In 1997, TECNOSSIS acquired its first offices outside of the Madrid area, in Barcelona, and in the same year, it formed a consortium with Microsoft which brought Windows NT to the market. This same year saw our expansion into the US, with our offices in Silicon Valley and Seattle. In 1998, we inaugurated our Barcelona offices, and the following year we took control of Argencom, the enterprise department store, as a strategic investment.


The year 2000 marked the beginning of major change in the company. It was the year in which the initial public offering of TECNOSSIS shares took place, with the aim of funding a more aggressive international expansion. With that initial public offering, our shares were put on the stock market.
