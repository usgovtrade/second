Cybersecurity EMI modules (EMIs) EMIs can be divided into two groups: * Discrete product modules. These are separate modules that do not have any other function but to protect the cybersecurity function. This is the easiest way to protect the firewall. * Integrated modules that are integrated in the firewall itself. These would have a bigger effect than the discrete modules, but they can also be tampered with easier. You can find a number of well-known and tested and tried EMI products. They have a huge range of firewall products that offer very different services for security, firewall, VPNs, content filtering, surveillance, and management tools. ZyXEL has a full-service support for their customers. ZyXEL UTM This is a firewall that can be used for any IPsec, IPSec VPNs, SSL VPNs, IPv6, L2TP, L3TP and has antivirus software. The firewall protects against spam, viruses, intrusion, and malware. ZoneAlarm Pro ZoneAlarm is an easy to use security software that helps the user to create an automatic defense against known and unknown threats. Check Point Software Technologies Check Point products are used to protect the networks and devices from attacks and protect them. Dell Dell has designed and developed a series of firewall products specifically for businesses.
