The idea was born from my need to encourage and challenge women who were in a mess and, after a year of fighting for my life and a near-death experience, I am more convinced than ever of the importance of the message within this book. _When You Meet the One_ offers a unique perspective of what it is like to be married. Most couples are familiar with some aspects of marriage but not others. What makes this book unique is that each story focuses on something different—how your partner deals with finances, whether he or she has the confidence to be the only breadwinner, how they choose to parent, how they deal with the challenges they encounter in life, etc. Although I have been married for twenty years, these issues, as I outlined in the introduction of this book, are also present in the lives of many of my friends and they have offered valuable insights to help mold this book.
