Sacred Heart School, Amritsar Sacred Heart High School is a private Catholic school located in Amritsar, Punjab, India. It was established by the De Nobili Fathers in 1919, as the convent-run Sacred Heart High School. In 1984, the De Nobili Fathers handed over the school to its lay management, including a board of trustees. In 1996, the trustees appointed a lay principal, and the school was allowed to take admission from children of any caste. It is ranked among the top 10 schools in Punjab.
