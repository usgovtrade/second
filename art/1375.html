Parting Is Such Sweet Sorrow is the first LP release by the rock band, Whitesnake. The album has been remastered from the original tapes. The US edition includes the bonus track, "Just the Way It Is" which was recorded during Whitesnake's 1987 UK tour and originally released in 1988 as a 7" single.
