I am indebted to my husband, Mark Schreiber, whose ideas and illustrations were perfect for the final project, and his ability to think like a kid was invaluable to me. A big thank you also to my editors at Ulysses Press, Laura Shumaker and Laina Adler. Your patience, expertise, and unflagging enthusiasm helped make this book better than I'd ever dreamed. I owe so much to all of the kids and adults who read versions of the manuscript and provided so much valuable feedback: Erin Knotts, Amanda, David, Amy Chappell, Amy D'Arcy, Amy Davis, and Sami and Sareerie Lee. A big thank you to my cousin Jocelyn for our conversations about the power of books and teaching. Thanks also to all of the friends and colleagues who provided their expertise: Tara Soles, Chris Rider, Jana Chamblee, and Deanna Larson. I also thank my family for being so supportive of my writing: to my sisters, who are the best, and to my parents, and to my stepmother and mother-in-law for taking an interest in my writing journey. Thank you to all the booksellers who continue to find new readers, and particularly to the ones who have come up with great names for characters! To my friends, old and new, who inspire me, are fun to be around, and make me laugh: Jennifer G. Kowit, Nicole Miller, Dianna Smith, Julie Tuttle, Kathy McMillan, Amy Reeve Zimmer, Jenne Wakeland, Michelle D. Johnson, Megan McIver, and Beth Wiseman.
