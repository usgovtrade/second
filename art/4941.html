invention relates to wireless communication devices, and, more particularly, to wireless communication devices capable of transmitting/receiving both non-audio data and audio data. Description of Related Art There exist many types of wireless communication devices. Among these devices are mobile phones, smart phones, wireless personal digital assistants (PDAs), wireless headsets, wireless headphones, and other wireless devices. These devices wirelessly communicate through transmitting and receiving electromagnetic waves, such as radio waves. Further, wireless communication devices can have additional functionality. For example, wireless communication devices may have a camera to take photographs and a display to display images, such as a screen. However, these devices are limited. Improvements in wireless communication devices can be beneficial to consumers.
