1. Technical Field The present invention relates to an electronic device including a flexible display. 2. Description of the Related Art Electronic devices have displays so that users can conveniently use the electronic devices. The electronic devices have been developed to provide functions in accordance with user needs while maximizing portability. Accordingly, flexible display technology for electronic devices, in which the displays are thin and pliable, is needed.
