I Like Revenge" is a song by the English progressive rock band Pink Floyd, written by Syd Barrett for the band's 1967 debut album The Piper at the Gates of Dawn. The full-length version of the track (7:20) was included as a bonus track on the 2009 reissue of the album, after being omitted from the original vinyl.
