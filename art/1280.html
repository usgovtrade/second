Some critics labeled it as a standout track, while others highlighted it as one of his most catchy songs. Promotion and release "We Can't Get Enough" was first heard as a snippet in one of Dev's Instagram posts. While the full song was leaked on YouTube. "We Can't Get Enough" was released on August 22, 2016. Composition The song was written by Dev, and produced by Dev and Eric Hudson, who also co-wrote the song with Mike Will Made It, DJ Spinz, Kyle "Smoove" Forbes and J. "Yung Dolf" Duron. Instrumentally, "We Can't Get Enough" is a trap and pop song. Dev explains the meaning of the song in an interview with Billboard: "I think people would be shocked by what was really just a normal day in my life. I went to the studio, like every night for 12 years, from 12 till, like, 35, and then I just left my band. I gave up all of my day jobs. I don't miss a thing, ever. I really just think about who I am as an artist and how I want to create things. Just about trying to stay in the present, stay in the moment, have fun. And you know, people don't take that seriously. You know, what I do for a living is make music. You know? Like, this is what I do, and it's not supposed to be all these other things. So it was a really really fun journey for me. I'm not gonna take it seriously. I'm just going to make it what it is, you know? I don't have time for anything else. Like, not like "I'm just here to have fun". I mean, sometimes you can make it your job and take it too seriously. That's not what this is." Live performances "We Can't Get Enough" was performed live on the Jimmy Kimmel Live!. Remix A remix featuring American rapper Lil Uzi Vert, titled "We Can't Get Enough (Remix)" was released on November 5, 2016.
