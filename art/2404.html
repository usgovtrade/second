Horse Lords of Aethe - A casual night of horse racing in the Aeternica Province. All ages are welcome. Aeternica Province is a fantasy world based on horse racing, the use of chariots and horses. The province is split into 8 racetracks, a series of race days each with a certain number of events. There are 4 races a day in each track. The Aeternica Province is run on a simple betting system with 3:1 on wins and 3:1 on losses. There are prizes for 1st place, 2nd place, and last place for the day.
