Education Primary school Secondary schools Colleges/Universities The London School of Hygiene & Tropical Medicine (LSHTM) offers postgraduate (post) courses at this site. The School of Oriental and African Studies (SOAS), University of London is also located nearby. London Metropolitan University has its medical school on the site. Hospital In addition to the teaching hospital, the site has another facility, The Nightingale Hospital which provides care in a major emergency such as a road traffic collision. The specialist facility within the hospital offers treatment for burns and head injury. At the Nightingale Hospital one in five patients is treated for injuries caused by alcohol and illegal drugs. They can also provide the surgical care needed to stabilise gunshot and stabbing victims. The Emergency Department is an urgent care facility, which assesses and treats minor injuries such as wounds, injuries from falls, sports and other blunt trauma.
