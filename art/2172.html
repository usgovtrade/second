A Chapera Surprise_ (2013) * * * Detective Frank Cyr is called to the scene of a dead body, that of former Boston University professor James Whitman, whose face has been slashed. A local homeless woman found him. He had worked for decades as a professor of linguistics at BU, but was better known as a linguist and amateur archaeologist. Cyr comes to realize that in these days of the internet, this man had used an alias—someone had outed him and threatened his life. But why? And why did the killer choose a face-off? A puzzle begins to unfold in the grisly aftermath of the murder, one that leads Detective Cyr to uncover an international conspiracy between scientists, police and governments that is as chilling as it is complex.
