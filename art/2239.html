After using an e-vending machine for a while, I realized that it can make the customer very dissatisfied, because it would have so many more possibilities if only they allowed some creativity. The best example of this is the game of “pachinko” that many vending machines in Japan have. It is really funny to watch a poor and exhausted guy hit one of these vending machines a few times, and get a game. I think they should make these machines like this all over the world, with games and other fun stuff. And perhaps when they don’t have the game, we could encourage them to take a look at the rest of the buttons. So this is the project that my friend and I are currently working on. The goal is to create a vending machine with enough buttons to make it fun for everybody. We’re working in two cities in New Zealand, as well as our friends in South Africa, where we have already a working machine. When I found out the vending machine in my university’s building was closed during the day, I found it strange. In Japan they are open 24 hours a day, and we never consider the time while shopping there. I had to find out more. I also found a book, which can help to understand the culture of vending machines in Japan. There are few things I think the Japanese culture is really good at, and this is one of them. I’m not sure it’s all great, but most people can agree, it’s better to have too many vending machines than no vending machines at all. As you can see, there are a lot of different vending machines in Japan. If you walk a bit around a big Japanese city, you’ll notice they are very common. Not everyone likes them. This blog post talks about them, and shares a lot of images of them. Most people in Japan don’t know much about them, but in many places in the world they are part of everyday life, and very useful. In the last decade or so, Japan has adopted a kind of new technology that is still not widely used. The machines, or vending machines, as they call them in Japan, are an essential part of Japan’s culture. This is the main reason that I wanted to share with you a great book I found. The book is called “Ningyo: Japanese Food vending Machines”, and it is a book that talks about food vending machines. It is written by Kenji Fujishima, and has a total of 48 pages, and is only one of the chapters of the book. However, in this book you can read about some special machines in some special places like Tokyo’s Tsukiji market, Akihabara’s Denonko or a vending machine of a bar in Minami-Ku, Shizuoka. Since I’ve never been to Japan, this is a really great book to get you some ideas. I know these machines are everywhere, but not everyone has the chance to discover them. Japan is really different from most of the places in the world. They have many different cultures, and a way of life that involves all these different cultures. This makes the culture of Japan more interesting, and in this post I want to share with you one of those interesting things. This is about the way Japanese people spend their lunch time. Japan has this little thing called a “kushikatsu”, which translates to “conveyor belt”. It’s not really a conveyor belt, though. It’s more like a restaurant that has lots of chefs. You take a seat at a table and you take your food from the restaurant chef in front of you. I loved this lunch because it allowed me to explore the restaurant and interact with the chef, and it’s a very Japanese kind of restaurant. They are all over the city, and they have so many different kinds of food that you have probably never seen or heard of before. It was a fun day and very different. The restaurant is called “kushikatsu” because the name is “kushikatsu” and the place is in fact, where kushikatsu comes from. Here’s some pictures: The restaurant is located at the fourth floor of the mall at Odaiba, and you can eat as many different things as you want. The chef will even create a kushikatsu plate for you. The interesting thing about this place is that all of the food is free, but you have to order something. So if you’re not into food, or don’t want to pay the price, you don’t have to. When I visited the restaurant I found a lot of different kinds of food. For example, this is a picture of a plate with fried gyoza and green tea jelly: The way the meal is designed allows you to choose whatever kind of food you want to eat. You can share it with your friends, and you can see all of the food on a big table. This is a great place to enjoy, if you’re in Japan or if you have friends or family who live in Japan. It’s a very Japanese experience that is so different, because every culture has its own ways of life. There is a lot to do at the mall at Odaiba, but this is definitely one of the best things you can do. It’s very exciting, and you can find things to do all over the place. And to top it off, it’s free and just a little bit quirky. I hope you can see how fun it is to visit a place like this, and see how much fun it can be to try something new. Enjoy life in the best way you can, and explore what’s out there, and see what amazing things are out there to be discovered.
