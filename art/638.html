Jair knew he could take out the big man right then, but he knew that would probably alert the men inside to his presence. He decided to wait, but he needed a plan. He pointed it toward a crack on the wall, watching in the dim light the men of Thildin's army as they marched toward the center of the cave. The man on the far left of the entrance noticed Jair.
