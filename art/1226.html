In the 'It's All About You' section, the lyrics talk about how to win back their partner and 'make it right' even after it ended. While not having any live performances, this song has achieved notable success in its digital sales at music stores in South Korea, selling approximately 250,000 digital copies as of 2018. Music video The official music video of "It's All About You" was released on May 22, 2012 at midnight KST through Cube Entertainment's YouTube account. Filmed in an alley, the video features several different couples and focuses on how they can move on and get over a failed relationship. Commercial performance "It's All About You" is B.A.P's first release to enter the Gaon Digital Chart, ranking at number 21 on the chart on the issue dated June 6, 2012. It also entered the Gaon Streaming Chart and ranked at number 11 on the chart. It peaked at number 8 on the Gaon Social Chart for the months of July to September.
