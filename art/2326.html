Here's what the average cost of a move looks like. Do Ask For References We wouldn't hire a contractor to do work for us if we hadn't interviewed their references. The same holds true for hiring a moving company. Find out what their previous clients had to say about their experiences with them. Don't Be Emotional About Your Valuables Most moving companies will not accept responsibility for damages incurred in transit. If something happens, you should expect to deal with the situation in-house, and not with the moving company. Don't Forget the Insurance If there's any question, make sure you get a copy of the insurance policy for each moving company you consider. That way you will know how much coverage your belongings are actually receiving. Don't Ask For An Estimate When The Truck Arrives Most likely, you are already prepared to move, and you want the work to start as soon as possible. With that said, it is not in your best interest to rush a moving company into providing an estimate. That will put you at risk of overpaying for services. Do Get Your Possessions Labeled Not sure what to do with your possessions once you've moved them to your new home? Get some stickers, and use them to label your possessions. Make sure to stick them on every item. Do Ask For A Discount When Doing the Job In Bulk Some movers will be more than willing to help you save money on shipping costs. Ask for a discount on the move when you are relocating large amounts of items. Do Not Pay In Advance Do not pay a company in advance. You should not be expected to do so. If they need the money, they will ask for it when the job is done. Do Not Give Any Extra Fees You should expect to pay for mileage, food, and lodging if the movers bring them. Do not give anything in advance. Do Ask For a Good Understanding of Terms There are several factors that may affect the total cost of your move, like fuel surcharges, miles, and other moving issues. For example, some movers charge extra fees for long-distance moves. Make sure that your moving company has clarified all of these details. Do Choose a Moving Company You Can Trust Be sure to choose a moving company that will not be shy about asking questions. If you're not sure about what the moving company will do, make sure you discuss it.
