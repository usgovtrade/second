Joe's Bar and Grill offers a full menu, featuring American specialties and gourmet sandwiches. This restaurant is a favorite place for lunch on the east side and for coffee, dessert and lunch on the west side. The Bistro at the DoubleTree features European cuisine including grilled items, chicken and pasta specialties. The SouperBowl offers soup and salad. The Dining Room serves a children's menu and also has a full bar. The Lobby Lounge serves specialty drinks and light snacks. The Grill at the DoubleTree serves hamburgers, flank steak and kung pao chicken. In the hotel: The Fountain Room Coffee Shop is open for breakfast, lunch and snacks. The Tea Lounge is open for tea, coffee, specialty drinks and snacks. The Lobby Bar is a full service bar and lounge with a large selection of beer and wine. The Garden Bar is open for snacks and happy hour. The Terrace Room is a casual eatery. The Pavilion is open for continental breakfast and lunch and is located in the lobby of the hotel. The Courtyard Cafe is a self-serve grab and go eatery. Casino The hotel offers free shuttle service to the casino, which is about a ten-minute drive away. Rooms All of the rooms are spacious and feature a work desk, workstation with two separate outlets, coffee maker, 32-inch LCD television and free local calls, voice mail and Internet access. The rooms are equipped with a safe, iron and ironing board, hair dryer, clock-radio, telephone with voicemail, and air conditioning. Guests may choose either a king-sized or double bed. Other amenities include a refrigerator and microwave. Sofas and chairs are also included. The hotel has a fitness center and indoor pool. For a nominal fee, guests may use the onsite business center.
