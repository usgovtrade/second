One Armed Dude and Three Moms One Armed Dude and Three Moms (also known as An O.G., a Three Moms, and A.G.I.D.) is a 2004 short film starring Andy Milonakis, Steve-O and his mom Dabney Coleman. The film is centered around Dabney Coleman's character, Mr. Kessler, who tries to escape from his mother and her boyfriend, who is the three-moms-guy of the film.
