Joe's Bar and Grill offers iced tea and sparkling wine by the glass or pitcher. The bar serves tap beer, wine, liquor and cocktails, and recently added specialty drinks like frozen margaritas and spiked orange juice drinks. It also serves small plates. The venue seats 175, offering a performance space for small events or private parties. It also features a dining room. It has a terrace that can host special events.
