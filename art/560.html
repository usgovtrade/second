Joe's Bar and Grill offers iced coffee! Joe's Bar and Grill was first opened on October 21, 2003. Every Thursday at Joe's Bar and Grill is Chicken N' Noodles Night. For the month of April 2013, Joe's Bar and Grill held a Food and Beverage Festival and held a chili contest. When Joe's Bar and Grill first opened, there was a limited number of people on the weekends. Now, there are many more people working and hanging out at Joe's Bar and Grill, since it is no longer a hidden gem. Joe's Bar and Grill had two banners to hang on the walls of the building. Joe's Bar and Grill was once called Joe's Bailiwick. Joe's Bar and Grill was featured in the first season of the reality show The Amazing Race.
