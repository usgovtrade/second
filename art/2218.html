My Kisses Are Very Private” We know you would be tempted to post your video, your love song, your kiss on the “what I would like to see on a site such as ours” page, right? I was too until I actually did it and realized that there is a more subtle, un-public place on our site to do so. We wanted to give our members a very simple way to put an extra “special” touch on their listing that didn’t seem “tacky” so we created a special spot called My Profile Page, found on the My Property tab. The “My Profile Page” is reserved for users to write something very special about themselves, but also as an area that is very private and only viewable by members. We don’t encourage users to write long, detailed accounts about themselves as it could come off as very self-involved. If your page doesn’t grab the right eyeball it can be very off putting. When you write something on your profile page it gives people who look at the listing even more reasons to think about you before they sign up. The beauty is that this is completely optional and can be left blank if you don’t want to write anything extra or you don’t have anything to say. If you want to write something just add something like this: Hi, my name is [your name here] and I love dogs! I am looking for a roommate who loves animals just as much as me.
