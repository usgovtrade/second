Get to Gettin' Up!" was the first radio-only single and the first single from the album to be released to mainstream radio; it has also become the first certified single in country music. It was originally planned for release on January 23, 2006, but an undisclosed issue with the song delayed its release to March 3, 2006. A second radio-only single, "Redneck Woman" (July 2006), also did not achieve airplay success, and thus it was not released to radio. It was released as a digital download on October 3, 2007.
