Back to the Beach" is a song performed by American hip hop duo The Super 3. It was the debut single by The Super 3 from their album Super 3, with production provided by American musician and producer, Jim Beanz. It was released for digital download and retail by Asylum Records on August 12, 2013. "Back to the Beach" is The Super 3's first number one hit on the US Billboard Dance/Electronic Songs chart and is the group's first charting single in seven years, following "Say (All I Need)" from 2007, that peaked at number seventy-six on the Billboard Hot 100.
