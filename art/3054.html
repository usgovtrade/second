It's Funny When People Cry" is featured on the track list. The movie's soundtrack album was released on February 4, 2002, by Jive Records. The album's lead single "Where Is the Love" peaked at number one on the US Billboard Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Singles Sales. The second single "One More Chance (remix)" peaked at number two on the US Billboard Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Singles Sales chart, and number 31 on the Hot 100. The third single "Just Go Girl" peaked at number seven on the US Billboard Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Singles Sales. The soundtrack did not make it to number one on the US Billboard Top 200, however it peaked at number three on the Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums chart, and number four on the Top Soundtracks.
