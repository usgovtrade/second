Marquesan Vacation Rental Often called the Marquesas Islands, there are eight unique archipelagos located here. Each island offers a unique combination of people, terrain, terrain and climate, and wildlife. Some of the islands are steep with towering trees at the top and the entire island is covered in shrub and tree vegetation. While other islands are flat and treeless, with just a little grass growing in a few places. Each island has its own rich history that can only be learned when you spend time on them. The best time to go to the Marquesas is December through May, because that’s when the weather is the warmest and driest. This is also when most of the boats leave. Marquesas Islands A 3 Night/4 Day Trip This adventure begins in the morning at Nuku Hiva island, where you will be picked up by your captain and taken on a catamaran to Motu Tocho. Next, visit Motu Tocho, Tongariki, Motu Ura, Motu Nui, Anapai, Teao, Motu Teavaenikai, and Motu Kao. A picnic lunch will be served on each island. Spend the afternoon sailing back to Nuku Hiva to return to our office to end the day. This adventure begins in the morning with a transfer to Motu Nui by car. You will be driven to Pokaikai Bay, the entrance to the Motu and are welcome to take a kayak to explore. In the afternoon, visit Motu Hauo and Motu Arai. After a picnic lunch you will be driven back to the beach and bade a fond farewell to the team.
