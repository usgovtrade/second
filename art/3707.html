His last three books of essays— _When You Play The Classics_ , _Invisible Republic_ , and _The Old, Weird America—_ were hailed by _New York_ magazine as "exhilarating . . . a new literary form . . . the kind of book you live with a pencil and the eraser nearby." His work has appeared in _The New York Times, Rolling Stone, The Nation_ , and _The Village Voice_. He teaches in the Graduate Creative Writing Program at New York University and is the curator of the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame and Museum's Writer's Symposium.
