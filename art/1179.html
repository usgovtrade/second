Why Aren't You Swimming?! is a unique and funny film filled with adventure, discovery and action. It is filmed with hand held cameras and follows a group of teenage boys who compete for the title of Flipperman. The film captures the intensity and the comradery of the competitive swimmers during the course of the swim season in the final days before the big qualifying event for the city wide swim meet. The film's theme music is Flipperman by Weird Al Yankovic and the film was shot entirely on a home-built 35mm camera in NYC.
