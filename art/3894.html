History 2010-2011: No One Does It Like You No One Does It Like You was released on April 12, 2011. In support of the album, the band headlined a tour across the United States. The second single, "Trust Me", has become quite successful on the mainstream charts, peaking at number 19 on the Billboard Hot 100 and also topping the airplay charts. The album's third single, "Shake" was released digitally on June 29, 2011. On June 29, 2011, No One Does It Like You was released on CD in Australia as a part of Sony BMG's new artist series. 2012: Beautiful World EP On June 27, 2012, Beautiful World EP was released as a digital download and on CD in North America. The EP features the title track, "Beautiful World", "Friends With You" and "The Best Forever". The track "The Best Forever" is an acoustic version. On June 28, 2012, Beautiful World was released in Australia as a digital download only. 2013: Beautiful World Tour On April 2, 2013, The Swon Brothers opened the Red Soil Tour alongside Plain White T's and G.R.L.. 2014: The Wasteland The Wasteland, a compilation album including remakes of past hits and the track "Sorrow" was released on April 9, 2014. 2015: Heartache The song "Hold Me Tonight" from The Wasteland is used in the ABC Family show Make It or Break It in an episode titled "Locker Room Romance" that aired on June 10, 2015. 2019: New Year's Eve The band released a holiday album titled New Year's Eve, containing two new songs and a cover of The Jackson 5 song "Never Can Say Goodbye" on December 23, 2019. It was also available as a free download in the iTunes store.
