Chapter 55: Gonna Fly 56. Chapter 56: End of the World Characters Aizen Academy students Chairperson Iba - A female first-year who is very energetic, kind, and helpful to her friends. When she saw the Homunculi and Hollows coming, she hid in a locker and was found by the main characters. She becomes the first sacrifice for Yumichika's execution and is put into a mental hospital. After her release, she runs into Hiyori. After being forced to deal with the events in the school, she transfers to another school. She also becomes friends with Marechiyo and Kusano. Chairperson Sasaki - A female first-year, who has a habit of drinking too much alcohol and is easily influenced by others. She becomes the second sacrifice for Yumichika's execution and is put into a mental hospital. After her release, she marries Kusano and becomes a mother. In the alternate future, she is killed by Yumichika during the 100th battle and Marechiyo buries her at the same tomb as the first sacrifice, Masaki Iba. Chairperson Nagi - A first-year male, who is very skilled at sports and often competes with others. He is always being accused of looking down on other people, as shown by Haruna's statement, but he's really a nice person. He does not participate in any events during the time skip due to a knee injury and is absent in chapter 32. Later, he is forced to be the sacrifice for Shun's execution. After dying, he is the seventh Homunculi victim, who was killed by Mashiro. He was buried next to the other sacrifices in the graveyard. Chairperson Yamato - A male second-year, who is very kind and easy going. He is a member of the Fist of Justice group. When Giemon reveals himself to the student body, he becomes a sacrifice in a trial for Yumichika's execution. At first, he is angered when Yumichika says he won't fight him, but he changes his mind once Yumichika reveals his reasoning for his actions. He is the fifth sacrifice in the mental hospital, but survives and later gets transferred to a mental hospital in another city. Later he is forced to die in a war with the Homunculi, but survived to have a daughter. It is revealed that he was alive and well in chapter 58, which means that he faked his death to go on a trip. Masaki Iba - A female third-year, who is the first sacrifice for Yumichika's execution. Her fate is unknown as she dies in a mental hospital. She may be living happily ever after in Heaven.
