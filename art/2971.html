Why Aren't You Swimming?! is a children's book in verse. It is written by Rafe Martin and illustrated by Jane Chapman and was published in 2005 by Little, Brown in the U.S. and was also released as an audio CD from the same publisher. The story was first published in the U.S. in the summer of 2002 and subsequently in the UK in 2003. It was followed by a second book Why Aren't You Singing? and then a third Why Are You Not Laughing?
