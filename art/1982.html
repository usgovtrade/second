Why Would You Trust Me? It is a well-known fact that not every business is what it seems. The appearance may deceive. Many corporations have good intentions. However, they do things that are not fair to the market and customers alike. As a result, they end up losing their business. They end up losing customers as well. If I promise to do something good to you, how do you know it is not a scam? Is that a fair question to ask? Yes! It is fair to question it. But trust should be earned. Trust is when a person believes that you are acting in their best interest. That you are worthy of them trusting you. In the end, a company’s reputation is the one thing that is the hardest to erase. Would you do business with your friend, who doesn’t fulfill your requests? It is also the same with an online business. What do You Want? When a person creates an online business, he will spend his hard earned cash to create that business. If the business does not make him happy or if he is not getting a return on his investment then he will be looking for someone else to invest in. However, if he loves what he is doing and there is a good return on investment, he will not look for a different investment as that will mean giving up an opportunity that many people would like to have. If you are unhappy with your online business, you will lose a customer. You will lose trust. A trust of the mind Trust is very hard to understand. It can be done once, but again it cannot be taken away and that is how it is meant to be. The only way to make a new relationship is to give trust. However, no one knows how long a trust of the mind will last, it is purely up to each person. The person who will always try to keep their promises and meet deadlines is a person who will be trustworthy. The person who is dishonest will lose that trust of the mind. Trust is something that cannot be traded. Trust is something that only comes through time and repeated efforts. Trust is like a new car. You put it on the road for the first time, and no matter how well you drive, no one will trust you until you’ve had several accidents. At this point they will trust you, just to make sure you don’t have any more accidents. Conclusion There are many websites and businesses to choose from. The good ones are hard to find but so are the scams. If a company is being sued for unfair business practices, they will be scams. These types of companies that take advantage of people will get caught in the end, however. Sometimes these companies do a good job at first but they are dishonest and cheat you once they get their profit. If you are looking for a trustworthy website to do your online shopping, you need to try a trustworthy company. It is better to go with an online seller who has been around for a long time and not a new company that just started up. Just keep your eyes open. If something is not right with the business, your instincts should let you know. The more the you follow that feeling, the sooner you will start seeing results. In the end, trust is not something you earn, but something you decide to give. It is hard to build trust but it is easy to lose it if you decide not to use it. When you decide to give it, you are a very lucky person indeed. If you have already read this article, you must have already opened your mind and have understood what you should expect from a website.
