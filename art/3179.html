Head of the Snake" (album) "Head of the Snake" is a song by Japanese band Crystal Kay. It was released on May 21, 2008, as the second single for their third album "Head of the Snake" on their label Awa Mania. "Head of the Snake" was a song used as the ending theme for anime Black Lagoon. Music video A music video was also produced for the song and was shown on various websites. In the video, there are three boys dressed in black suits. They each sit in an airplane in an empty warehouse, while the lights are turned off and a white flash plays onscreen. Later, they move around in a large, open room with flashing lights.
