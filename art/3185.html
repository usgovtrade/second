Cybersecurity EMI modules Information System and Cybersecurity EMI modules are elective or subject-based academic modules that are offered in Years 3, 4 and 5 of the undergraduate curriculum. The modules were introduced in September 2011. EMI was developed to allow learners to study subjects in areas they are most interested in and can lead to a first degree in a subject that they enjoy and is related to their future aspirations.
