Make Some Magic Happen", "Make A Mark", "Live & Learn", "Life's Not A Struggle", "Live To Love", "Love Somebody", "Make Today Count" and "Shine Your Light". The song "Make Some Magic Happen" was used as the theme song to the 2008 film The Curious Case of Benjamin Button. It was also used in the 2011 film Fast Five and in an episode of Bones. The song "Life's Not A Struggle" was used in the 2013 film The Purge and on an episode of Grey's Anatomy. In March 2013, the album was made available in download format on the iTunes Store in France.
