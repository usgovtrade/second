Variable annuity life Insurance can not be cancelled. You are not able to buy an annuity that you sell your Annuity for a lump sum. You are not able to change the amount of annuity you receive if you make this change within 5 years from the date of annuity purchase. You can not change the payments unless you renew the policy for 10 years. You are not able to buy an annuity if you have a pre-existing illness. The minimum and maximum payments that can be made are different when compared to a normal life Insurance policy. The rate of Return is also higher. For example, if the minimum and maximum payments are $1 and $10 respectively, if the cost of $10 insurance is $100, then you have to pay $101 for the annuity and for every dollar you pay $1.1. Most importantly, how long will this investment last? If you buy a Life Insurance Policy for $10,000, it will definitely not last until the day you die, let alone for 120 years. But if you buy an annuity that provides $120 every month until you die, this might last you a lifetime. And this amount you will get will depend on the time of your investment. How annuity can be very profitable? Annuity is considered as the best way to save money, because it has long-term interest. It can be a very profitable and worthwhile investment. But one should be wise enough to take the right decision at the right time. For example, in the early year of your investment, you will get high amount of return, but after that your money will be eroded and it will reach a very low level. You have to make an effort to know your risk level and find the right choice.
