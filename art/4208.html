Oberlin Review Oberlin Review is a nonpartisan and nonprofit journal dedicated to a free press. It was founded in 1988 by the Oberlin College journalism program as a quarterly. Articles deal with current events, books and films, and include contributions by well-known authors, journalists and other writers. Students are encouraged to submit.
