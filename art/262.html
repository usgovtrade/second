Joe's Bar and Grill offers a restaurant, bar, lounge and banquet room. The building is located in the heart of the city at 25 S. Madison Street, at the corner of Washington Street and Jackson Street. Built in 1853, the historic building has undergone a complete restoration and now boasts high ceilings, intricate woodwork, original columns and stately chandeliers. The decor of the main room is reminiscent of Victorian era with colors of soft browns and tans. One cannot help but notice the beautiful parquet flooring which runs throughout the room. The restaurant seats between 40 and 50 and opens to the bar, which can seat another 50 and is located adjacent to the large meeting room, where weddings and other private events are hosted. Joe's offers a full bar with three large screens TVs mounted above the bar, seven high definition TVs and a balcony which seats 35, offering another venue for private events. In the banquet room, the tables are set for up to 200 people. The banquet room has views of both Washington and Jackson Streets, and has sloped walls, which allow the sound from the meeting room to carry throughout the banquet room. The meeting room and the banquet room can be combined for weddings, with the banquet room serving as the dance floor.
