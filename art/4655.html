Dallas County Clerk's Office seeks volunteer workers to help during the May Primary Election April 16, 2018 Dallas County Clerk John Warren is seeking volunteers to serve as poll judges and registration deputies for the May 22, 2018 General Election and November 6, 2018 General Election. Poll workers and registration deputies must be U.S. Citizens, have current Texas Drivers licenses or state ID’s and be registered to vote. Poll workers will serve as liaison between the voters and Election Inspectors. All volunteers must attend a Volunteer Orientation meeting at 10:30 a.m. on April 30 at the Dallas County Administration Building, 600 Commerce Street, Dallas. Space is limited. Please bring your most recent driver’s license or state ID as proof of identification. A training packet with important information will be sent to those who volunteer. For more information or to volunteer, please call (214) 819-5940 or email elections@dallascounty.org. This entry was posted on Tuesday, April 17th, 2018 at 2:11 pm and is filed under Uncategorized.
