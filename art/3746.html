If you’re looking for a way to kick off your summer more than 30 feet above the ground, then check out the Skywalk over the San Diego River. The Skywalk, near the San Diego Trolley station at 4th Avenue and Ash, is open through Sept. 30. The Skywalk stretches more than 40 feet above the San Diego River, with a glass floor and handrails. The cost is $2 for adults, $1 for seniors, $1.50 for children, and $5 for families. To get there, take the Trolley to 4th and Ash. Just off 4th Avenue, between the Trolley and the river, is a walkway that will take you to the Skywalk. You can also take the Riverwalk to 4th Avenue, and look for a set of stairs and walkway that leads you to the Skywalk. To learn more, visit their Facebook page.
