Kill or Be Killed Kill or Be Killed (released as Killing Floor in North America) is a 2005 South Korean crime film written and directed by Ryu Seung-wan. Plot The movie begins with a robbery at a convenience store. A man with no eyes, who calls himself "Eyes", is found on the road after he was run over by a car. It's revealed later in the film that this man is the owner of a local dojo, and used to be a very honorable police officer. The current police chief and the dojo owner were once lovers, but a tragic accident resulted in her death, and the two split up after that. Eyes tells the tale of how he became the dojo's owner to the son of the police chief and the dojo owner's ex-lover, who is now his son's fiance.
